Meet the dedicated professionals behind RegenAsia Hub

Implementing Team

Our core team from Practical Action is responsible for developing and managing the RegenAsia Hub platform, curating knowledge resources, and fostering community engagement.

Ayan

Chief Curator

Practical Action

Ayan leads the RegenAsia Hub initiative, bringing extensive experience in agricultural knowledge management and community building. With a background in sustainable agriculture and digital platforms, Ayan oversees the strategic direction and content development for the hub.

Arunabha

Lead Curator

Practical Action

Arunabha specializes in regenerative agriculture practices across South Asia. With deep knowledge of traditional farming methods and modern sustainable approaches, Arunabha curates resources and case studies that highlight effective practices and successful models from the region.
